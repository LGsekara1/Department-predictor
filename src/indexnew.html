The table on the left gives the statistics (minimum GPA required in the respective years) of how BSc. Engineering (Hons.) students were seggregated into departments of specialisation based on their prefernce and the first semester performance (GPA)

The BSc. Engineering (Hons.) degree program of the university of moratuwa recruits students through the G.C.E A/L examination under physical science stream which is held annually. After completing the first semester which is for a duration of 14 academic weeks of studying general principles of engineering under common discplines, they are evaluated through continuous and written assesments. Then, based on a graded point average (GPA) system their academic progress is credited.

There onwards they are familiarized with the specialization fields which they can select for further study. The undergraduates are thoroughly guided by the academic staff and the career guidance unit of the university to select the most suitable department based on their interest and the academic performance. Though there is a common misconception that those with higher academic performance should follow displines like ENTC and CSE, that is actally a taboo. As we witness many of those who have excelled in each discipline in the modern technologically evolving world, it is with great resposibility that we state that passion and interest in the displine should be the main focus in applying for department selection.
